Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Data Collection

AnonChat X does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We do not require phone numbers, emails, or any personally identifiable information to use the app. All messages and data are stored only on your device.

2. End-to-End Encryption

All messages sent via AnonChat X are end-to-end encrypted, ensuring that only you and the recipient can read them. We do not have access to your messages or any other communication.

3. Self-Destructing Messages

You can set messages to automatically delete after a selected time. Once deleted, they are permanently removed from your device and cannot be recovered.

4. Permissions

To function properly, the app may request access to:

  • Storage: To save and send encrypted messages.
  • Camera & Photos: If you choose to send images.
  • Notifications: To alert you about new messages.

However, this data is never collected, stored, or shared with third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

AnonChat X does not use third-party analytics, ads, or tracking tools. We do not share any data with advertisers or external services.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with the latest update date.
